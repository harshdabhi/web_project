About The Firm

Who are we ?

ACME Manufacturing Corp is specializes in car assembly. ACME Manufacturing has three buildings and six different machines to assemble various parts for car manufacturing. ACME Manufacturing opened in 2001 and now has nearly 1,000 employees.

The Web Application

This web application allows each worker at ACME Manufacturing to have a view of the machines and their operating status. It thus facilitates maintenance and monitoring of machine repairs.
